.. math::
    :label: min_wall

    t_m = \frac{PD}{2(S_m+yP)} + a

Where:
    .. math:: t_m = \text{Minimum required thickness} [inch]
    .. math:: P = \text{Design pressure} [psi]
    .. math:: S_m = \text{Material allowable at design temperature} [psi]
    .. math:: D = \text{Pipe outer diameter} [inch]
    .. math:: y = \text{Factor} (default=0.4)


Sample Calculation
==================

Inputs
------

.. jupyter-execute::
    :hide-code:

    %%render symbolic
    P = 1000*psi;       # system design pressure
    D = 12.75*inch;     # pipe diameter
    t = 0.375*inch;     # pipe thickness

Results
-------

.. jupyter-execute::
    :hide-code:

    %%render long
    sigma_p = (P*D)/(2*t);  # pressure stress [hoop]
